Ingredients:
 - 200gm fresh mushrooms
 - 2 fresh tomatoes
 - 2 teaspoons ginger garlic paste
 - 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
 - 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
 - 1 teaspoon red chili powder
 - 1 teaspoon onion powder
 - 3-4 teaspoons maggie magic masala
 - 1/2 cup schezwan chutney
 - 1/2 cup water
 - 1/4 cup olivr oil
 - Salt to taste

Directions:
 1. Add oil to the hot pan.
 2. Add mustard seeds and onion powder.
 3. As the seeds start to pop up, add turmeric and chili powder.
 4. Add pieces of tomatoes and mix it well.
 5. Add ginger garlic paste, maggie magic masala, schezwan chutney, salt to taste and mix it well.
 6. Add 1/2 cup of water and cover the pan with lid and simmer for 2-3 minutes.
